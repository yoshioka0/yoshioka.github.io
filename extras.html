Common Noun Examples

Examples of common nouns include 人 (hito – person), 町 (machi – town), 車 (kuruma – car).

×

Noun Conjugation

Although Japanese nouns don’t conjugate like verbs, they can change form based on particles, e.g., の (no), が (ga), へ (e).
