What are Japanese Nouns?

Nouns in Japanese are words used to represent people, things, places, ideas, etc. They are usually the subject of a sentence.

Examples of Japanese Nouns

  • 犬 (いぬ, inu) – Dog
  • 猫 (ねこ, neko) – Cat
  • 学校 (がっこう, gakkou) – School
